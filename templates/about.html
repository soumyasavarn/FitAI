About FitAI

Discover how FitAI revolutionizes personal fitness through cutting-edge technology and smart analytics.

Our Features

  • Activity Tracking: Track steps, calories, and distance.
  • Goal Setting: Set and achieve personalized fitness goals.
  • Predictive Analysis: Use AI to predict fitness achievements.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Sleek and easy-to-use interface.
  • CS50 Integration: Developed with the robustness of Harvard's CS50 principles.
  • Flask Framework: Built on the powerful Flask web framework.
  • Flask-Session: Secure session management across requests.
  • Requests: Real-time data interaction via external APIs.
  • Cachelib: Enhanced performance with effective caching strategies.
  • Google Generative AI: Personalized fitness advice using AI technology.
  • Pillow: Advanced image processing capabilities.

Why Choose FitAI?

FitAI is not just an app; it's a companion that understands your fitness needs and evolves with you. Whether you're looking to shed a few pounds, improve your overall health, or train for an upcoming event, FitAI provides all the tools and insights you need for success.

Our application combines state-of-the-art technology with user-friendly design to ensure that your fitness journey is as engaging and effective as possible. With FitAI, every step you take brings you closer to your fitness goals.
